About Eddie Dee Energy Resources Limited

Eddie Dee Energy Resources Limited (also trading as Eddie Dee Petroleum Services Co) is a leading Nigerian oil and gas services company headquartered in Sagamu, Ogun State. Founded and led by experienced petroleum engineers, we bring world-class technical expertise to Nigeria's dynamic energy sector.

Our company is built on a foundation of engineering excellence, safety consciousness, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. We understand the unique challenges of operating in Nigeria's oil and gas industry and provide solutions that are both technically sound and commercially viable.

With our strategic location in Ogun State and deep knowledge of Nigerian petroleum operations, we serve clients across the upstream, midstream, and downstream sectors. Our team combines international best practices with local expertise to deliver exceptional results on every project.

At Eddie Dee Energy Resources, we don't just provide services—we build lasting partnerships with our clients, supporting their growth and contributing to Nigeria's energy independence.

Comprehensive Petroleum Engineering Services

Petroleum Engineering Consultancy

Expert technical advisory services covering reservoir engineering, production optimization, well design, and field development planning. Our petroleum engineers provide data-driven insights to maximize asset value and operational efficiency.

Energy Solutions & Supply

Reliable supply of petroleum products and energy solutions tailored to industrial, commercial, and institutional clients across Nigeria. We ensure consistent product quality and timely delivery.

Technical Training & Capacity Building

Health, Safety & Environmental Services
